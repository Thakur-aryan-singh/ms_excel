# Project: E-Commerce Product Pricing & Discount Analytics (Myntra Dataset)
Tools: Microsoft Excel (Power Query, Pivot Tables, Advanced Formulas, Interactive Dashboards)

Analyzed 81K+ e-commerce product records across multiple brands and categories, performing structured data cleaning, transformation, and exploratory analysis to evaluate pricing distribution, discount strategies, and category level performance.

Derived actionable insights including ~43.6% average platform discount, significant price variance between premium and mid-range brands, and identified high concentration of listings within a few dominant product categories, indicating demand driven assortment strategies.

Developed an interactive Excel dashboard using Pivot Tables, slicers, and dynamic charts to monitor brand level inventory distribution, category share, and discount effectiveness, enabling quick comparison of pricing and promotional patterns.
